# Services Offered

Our barbershop offers a wide range of professional grooming services to meet all your needs.

## Hair Services

### Haircut

- **Price**: $30
- **Description**: Professional haircut tailored to your style preferences. Includes consultation, cutting, and styling.

### Fade
- **Duration**: 45 minutes
- **Price**: $35
- **Description**: Precision fade haircut with multiple length transitions. Perfect for modern, clean looks.

### Design
- **Duration**: 45 minutes
- **Price**: $35
- **Description**: Custom hair designs, patterns, and artistic cuts. Includes intricate detailing and styling.

## Beard Services

### Beard Trim
- **Duration**: 15 minutes
- **Price**: $15
- **Description**: Professional beard trimming and shaping to maintain your desired look.

### Hot Towel Shave
- **Duration**: 20 minutes
- **Price**: $25
