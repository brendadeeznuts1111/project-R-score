# Pricing Guide

## Service Pricing

### Hair Services
- **Haircut**: $30 (30 minutes)
- **Fade**: $35 (45 minutes)
- **Design**: $35 (45 minutes)

### Beard Services
- **Beard Trim**: $15 (15 minutes)
- **Hot Towel Shave**: $25 (20 minutes)

### Specialty Services
- **Braids**: $50+ (60-90 minutes, price varies by complexity)
- **Facial**: $40 (30 minutes)

## Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:
- **CashApp**: Send payment to barber's CashApp handle
- **Venmo**: Send payment to barber's Venmo username
- **PayPal**: Send payment via PayPal.me link

## Tips

Tipping is appreciated but not required. Standard tip is 15-20% of service cost.

## Package Deals

Combine multiple services for discounted rates:
- Haircut + Beard Trim: $40 (save $5)
- Haircut + Hot Towel Shave: $50 (save $5)
- Full Service (Haircut + Beard Trim + Hot Towel): $60 (save $10)

## Payment Process

1. Select your services
2. Choose your preferred barber
3. Complete payment via QR code or payment link
4. Receive confirmation and join waiting room if needed

## Refunds

Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. Please contact your barber directly or reach out to support.
