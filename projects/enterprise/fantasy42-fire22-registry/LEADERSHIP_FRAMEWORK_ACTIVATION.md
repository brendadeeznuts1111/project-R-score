## üö® **Risk Mitigation & Contingency Plans**

### **Communication Risks**

- **Risk**: Department heads don't receive or acknowledge notifications
- **Mitigation**: Multiple communication channels (email, Slack, GitHub)
- **Contingency**: Personal follow-up calls and direct messaging

### **Technical Access Issues**

- **Risk**: GitHub access problems or CODEOWNERS configuration errors
- **Mitigation**: Pre-validation testing and backup access procedures
- **Contingency**: Temporary admin access and expedited IT support

### **Meeting Scheduling Conflicts**

- **Risk**: Scheduling conflicts prevent timely kickoff meetings
- **Mitigation**: Flexible scheduling and multiple time slot options
- **Contingency**: Virtual meeting options and recorded sessions

### **Understanding Gaps**

- **Risk**: Department heads don't fully understand their roles and
  responsibilities
- **Mitigation**: Detailed documentation and one-on-one clarification sessions
- **Contingency**: Extended onboarding period and mentorship assignments
