## üéì Key Concepts

### Shadow Graph
A graph representation of markets where nodes represent markets and edges represent relationships with correlation and latency metrics.

### Hidden Steam
Sharp money movements that occur in dark/API-only markets before becoming visible in the UI.

### Reverse Line Movement (RLM)
When public betting heavily favors one side but the line moves in the opposite direction, indicating sharp money.

### Shadow Arbitrage
Arbitrage opportunities that exist between visible and hidden markets.

### Advanced Detection System
49 components providing sophisticated detection and analysis capabilities.
